CalWriter Help

CalWriter keeps your projects organized in books with unlimited folders and chapters. Start a new book from the home page or open an existing one to continue writing.

  • Editing – Chapters open in tabs grouped by book. The rich text toolbar offers formatting, lists, undo/redo and find/replace. Notes and chapters save automatically.
  • Navigation – The sidebar tree can be collapsed or expanded and remembers its state. Drag and drop folders or chapters to rearrange them. Right click items to rename or delete.
  • Import/Export – Use Export Database to save a .calwdb archive or Download Database as a .zip for backups. Import Database merges an archive into your data.
  • Book Wizard – Quickly create a book with starter folders and the first chapter.
  • Stats and Search – Search across all books and track daily word counts on the Stats page.
  • Customization – Choose colors for book tabs, enable dark mode and adjust editor background in App Settings. Pre-edit mode lets you insert icon tags with a click.

Chapters can be exported to .docx files individually or combined from the book page. When you're done for the day, export or download your database to keep everything safe.
