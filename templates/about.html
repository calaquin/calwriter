About CalWriter

CalWriter began as a small experiment with Flask and quickly grew into a lightweight writing application. It was designed to keep your work organized in a simple folder structure while still providing basic word processor tools.

The goal is to let writers focus on their stories without complicated menus or cloud logins. All data lives in a persistent directory so you can rebuild the container or move it to another machine without losing any chapters.

CalWriter is open source and continually evolving with small improvements. Below is the full MIT License that governs its distribution.
